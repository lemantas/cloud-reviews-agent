# INSTRUCTIONS
1. **Identify Common Aspects**: Look for mentions of these typical cloud hosting aspects:
   - support, pricing_billing, verification_account_management, reliability, performance
   - usability_interface, provisioning_setup, docs_community, api_automation, networking

   - neutral_examples: up to 3 reviews with rating = 3
   - negative_examples: up to 3 reviews with rating ≤ 2

4. **Sort and Filter**:

   - Return top 10 aspects only
